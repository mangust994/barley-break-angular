How to play classic barley-break?

The popular puzzle "barley-break" was invented at the end of the 19th century. The classic playing field is a 4x4 matrix of cells, on which the numbers from 1 to 15 are located in order (from left to right and from top to bottom). The last cell is empty. The cells are shuffled in a certain way, and the player's task is to restore their original correct position. This can be done only by moving to an empty cell another cell adjacent to it (located to the left, right, above or below the empty one).
